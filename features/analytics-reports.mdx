## Sales analytics

### Income chart

Track daily revenue trends:

<Accordion title="Chart details">

  - **Updates**: Real-time as sales are processed
  - **Color**: Green gradient for positive revenue
</Accordion>

- Identify high-performing days

- Track revenue momentum

### Annual revenue chart

Visualize monthly revenue over time:

<Accordion title="Chart details">
  - **Type**: Bar chart
  - **Period**: Last 6 months
  - **Grouping**: Monthly totals
  - **Color**: Purple bars matching brand theme
</Accordion>

- Identify seasonal trends
- Track month-over-month growth

Monitor daily expenses:

<Accordion title="Chart details">

## Inventory analytics

### Stock status distribution

Understand your inventory health at a glance:

<Accordion title="Doughnut chart">
  Displays three categories:
  - **Stock available** (green): Products with adequate inventory
  - **Low stock** (purple): Products below threshold
  - **Out of stock** (light purple): Products with zero inventory
</Accordion>

**Actionable insights:**
- Percentage of products in each status
- Exact count of products per category
- Visual alert for restocking needs

### Product rotation analysis

Analyze how frequently products sell:

<Accordion title="Rotation categories">
  - **Very frequent**: Fast-moving items
  - **Frequent**: Regular sellers
  - **Infrequent**: Slow-moving inventory
</Accordion>

**Use this data to:**
- Optimize inventory levels
- Identify bestsellers
- Spot slow-moving products for promotions
- Make smarter purchasing decisions

<Warning>
Slow-moving inventory ties up capital. Use rotation data to run promotions or adjust stock levels.
</Warning>

## Performance indicators

Key business metrics calculated automatically:

### Formulas used

```
Average Order Value = Total Revenue ÷ Number of Orders

Growth Rate = ((Current Period - Previous Period) ÷ Previous Period) × 100

Inventory Turnover = Units Sold ÷ Average Inventory

Sales Velocity = Units Sold ÷ Days in Period
```

### Benchmarking

Compare your metrics against:
- Your historical performance
- Industry averages (when available)
- Custom goals you set

<Tip>
Set monthly revenue and transaction goals in settings. The analytics dashboard will show your progress toward those targets.
</Tip>
